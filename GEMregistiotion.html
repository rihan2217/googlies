GeM Registration – Start Selling to the Government of India

The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is an official procurement portal launched by the Government of India to simplify buying and selling for government departments, PSUs, and ministries.

  • Streamlines procurement procedures with complete transparency.
  • Offers a secure and convenient digital platform for government transactions.
  • Allows buyers and sellers to connect easily, facilitating faster and reliable trade.
  • Ensures compliance with government regulations for hassle-free participation.

Who Can Register on GeM?

  • Proprietorship Firms and LLPs
  • Private and Public Limited Companies
  • MSMEs and Startups
  • NGOs, Trusts, and Societies
  • Manufacturers, Distributors, Dealers, and Service Providers

Documents Required for GeM Registration

Entity Type Documents Needed
Proprietorship PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank details, address proof
Partnership / LLP Partnership deed, PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank details, GST certificate if applicable
Private / Public Limited Certificate of incorporation, MCA filings, PAN card, GST certificate, bank details
MSME / Startup Udyam registration certificate, MSME or Startup certificate, PAN card, bank details
NGO / Society Registration certificate, PAN card, bank details

Additional documents that may be required depending on your entity:

  • Trademark certificate (if applicable)
  • Cancelled cheque or bank passbook copy
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)

Step-by-Step Process of GeM Seller Registration

  1. Create your user profile on the GeM portal.
  2. Upload all necessary business and legal documents.
  3. Build and complete your product or service catalog.
  4. Submit your application and wait for verification.
  5. Start bidding on government tenders once approved.
